Our Story

Doral's First
Wood-Oven Pizzeria

Founded in 2005, Spizzigo brought the art of the wood-fired oven to Doral — blending Old World Italian tradition with the warmth and energy of Miami. Every dish, from our slow-simmered sauces to our hand-stretched doughs, is made with care, authenticity, and amore.

Whether it's a quick lunch, a date night, or a family celebration, our kitchen is always on fire and our table is always yours.
